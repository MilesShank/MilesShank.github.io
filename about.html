Hi! I'm Miles!
I am a full stack developer and visual designer. I graduated from Oberlin College in 2017.
My degree is in East Asian Studies, but ever since graduation I have been pivoting towards technology and design. In the past several years, I've designed for video games, limited merchandise runs, and a runway fashion show. Ever since doing work on my first video game project, CROSSNIQ+ for the Nintendo Switch, I have been transitioning from mostly art direction and asset production, to writing actual program code. Designing is something I love, whether it's a charming user interface, or a cleverly structured back end.
In my spare time I love to travel and create in other forms. In college I studied mandarin, so I am always taking any opportunity I can get to practice, at home, or abroad.
Aside from languages, I enjoy painting shoes, and designing my own clothing. Identity is important to me and I express that through my hobbies and through my appearance.
